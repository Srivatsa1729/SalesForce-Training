1. What is CRM?

CRM, also known as Customer Relationship Management, is a system designed to help companies handle customer interactions in a smarter way. Instead of maintaining records manually, businesses use CRM software to organize customer details, monitor conversations, and manage services efficiently.
With the help of CRM, organizations can:
Keep customer information in one place
Improve communication with clients
Monitor sales activities
Provide better customer support
Build long-term customer relationships


2. Why Do Organizations Use Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform that helps companies manage their business processes digitally. Many organizations choose Salesforce because it simplifies customer management and increases work efficiency.
Benefits of Salesforce:
Easy management of customer data
Access to information from anywhere
Automation of repetitive tasks
Improved teamwork and collaboration
Better tracking of sales and services
Secure storage of business records

3. Important Salesforce Concepts
Account
An Account is used to represent an organization or institution in Salesforce.
Example:
A university, company, or business organization


Contact

A Contact refers to an individual person connected to an Account.
Example:
A student or employee
This object contains personal details like:
Name
Email
Phone number
Address

Opportunity

An Opportunity represents a possible business activity or future process.
Example:
Student admission procedure
